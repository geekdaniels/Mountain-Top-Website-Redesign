Post UTME Screening Exercise

The Post UTME Screening Exercise of Mountain Top University, Ibafo comes up on the 29th and 30th of August, 2017 by 9a.m. prompt. You are to come with following documents:

For UTME Students

  1. O' Level Result (Original and 4 photocopies)
  2. UTME Result printout (Original and 4 photocopies)
  3. Receipt of Payment for MTU Screening
  4. Birth Certificate
  5. Passport Photograph with full Name and Signature of the Candidate on the reverse side of the Photograph

For Direct Entry Students

  1. The Candidate must have obtained Direct Entry Form from JAMB
  2. The Candidate must ensure that his/her Transcript from Candidate's institution is forwarded to the Registrar MTU

OUR VISION

The Mountain Top University is envisioned as a dynamic centre of academic excellence in the liberal tradition of excellence for the production of top-rate, morally sound graduates of distinctions who will be globally competitive for outstanding impact on the Nigerian societal and global development.

OUR MISSION

The mission of Mountain Top University is the provision of excellent facilities for the training of men and women in various academic, professional and vocational disciplines in an atmosphere that will enhance the simultaneous development of their spiritual, mental and physical faculties, in order to produce morally upright and academically sound graduates with specialised skills and value systems, who will be independent, invariably self-employable and who will be able to impact positively on the development (be it in leadership, academic, public and the industrial sectors) of their communities, the Nigerian society and the global community at large.
